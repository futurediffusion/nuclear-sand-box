Extortion flow v1

1. BanditGroupIntel enqueues one extortion intent in ExtortionQueue when group score crosses the extort threshold and cooldown/duplicate guards pass.
2. BanditGroupMemory intent becomes extorting immediately when the queue entry is created.
3. BanditWorldBehavior reacts to extorting: leader and bodyguards enter EXTORT_APPROACH toward last_interest_pos. Scavengers keep their own work.
4. BanditExtortionDirector consumes the queued intent, starts one runtime job, assigns leader + up to 2 nearest guards, and takes scripted control of them.
5. Assigned bandits move toward the player during approach.
6. Taunt phase: the first assigned bandit that reaches taunt range shows the extortion line and marks the job as taunted.
7. Choice phase: when an assigned bandit reaches collect range, the director opens the extortion choice bubble and waits for the player response.
8. Resolve:
   - pay -> spend gold -> mark resolved -> assigned bandits force_return_home -> AI re-enabled after a short delay
   - refuse -> warning strike phase -> taunt speaker approaches -> scripted melee warning strike -> mark resolved -> assigned bandits force_return_home
   - insult -> full aggro -> scripted control released -> normal combat AI takes over
   - close/dismiss choice externally -> treated as refuse -> warning strike
9. Abort conditions: missing group/leader/speaker, broken assigned composition, stronger group intent (for example hunting), or player too far. Abort closes the choice, releases control, and resets group intent to idle if it was still extorting.
10. Cleanup: resolved/aborted jobs are removed from _active_extortions on the next director tick. In-flight extortion jobs are runtime-only and are discarded on world/chunk rebuild.
